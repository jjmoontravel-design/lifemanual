Privacy Policy

Life Manual · Effective June 28, 2026

Life Manual ("the app", "we") is a daily life-skills learning app. We built it to respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and why. In short: almost everything stays on your own device, we don't sell your data, and we don't show ads or use third-party tracking.

1. Information stored on your device

Your progress, streaks, coins, chosen companion, schedule, settings, and completed lessons are stored locally on your device (in your browser/app storage). This information is not sent to us and we cannot see it.

2. AI companion chat

When you chat with your animal companion, the messages you type are sent to our secure server to generate a reply, then returned to you. We use this only to produce the response. We do not use your chat messages to build a profile of you, and we do not sell them. Please avoid sharing sensitive personal information in chat.

3. Payments

If you choose to buy a premium upgrade, payment is processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card details — Stripe handles them directly under their own privacy policy.

4. Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules notifications on your device to nudge you about lessons and events. You can turn these off any time in your device settings.

5. Information we do NOT collect

  • No account or login is required to use the app.
  • No advertising identifiers, no ad networks.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data with third parties for marketing.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app stores progress locally and does not require a child to provide personal details.

6. Children's privacy

Life Manual is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will remove it.

7. Data retention & deletion

Because your data lives on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app. Chat messages sent to our server are processed transiently to generate replies and are not retained for profiling.

8. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we'll revise the date at the top of this page.
